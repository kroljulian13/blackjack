There are many ways to play Blackjack game. The purpose of this site is to give you possibility to test game strategies before trying it in real casino. Assuming than you are going to play using basic strategy this site allows you to test variation of bets during the multiple games. When loosing you try to minimize the bets and when winning you try to maximize bets.
One calculation is a batch of games of 4 decks of cards. Blackjack game simulator is under development and has been implemeted following the simplified basic strategy, split and insurance are not yet implemented.
