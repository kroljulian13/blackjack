Concept

There are many ways to play Blackjack game. The purpose of this site is to give you possibility to test game strategies before playing in real casino. Assuming than you are going to play using basic strategy this site allows you to test variation of bets during the multiple games. Loosing you need to try to minimize bets and winning you need to try to maximize bets. You can achive it by selecting desired bet multipliers in consecutive games on page Strategy select => Bet Strategy.
One calculation is a batch of games of 4 decks of cards. Blackjack game simulator is under development and has been implemeted following the simplified basic strategy. Split and insurance are not yet implemented.
